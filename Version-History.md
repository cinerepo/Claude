# Version History

## Purpose

This document tracks the evolution of content in this repository over time.

I serve as a version analyst for this repo — reading every file, understanding what it contains, and maintaining awareness of how things change from commit to commit. Think of it like `grep` vs `find`: not just locating things, but understanding the meaning and context of what's there.

### What I track:
- **What exists** — every file, its purpose, and its content
- **What changed** — additions, removals, rewrites, and refactors between versions
- **Why it matters** — patterns, themes, and the direction the work is heading
